About Us

Welcome to Our Campus

Mother Teresa School, a Private English Medium, Co-Educational Institute, is situated at Mahishakola, Nazirpur, in the district of Nadia, West Bengal. Established on 21st April 2018, it is administered by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Krishnagar under the guidance of Rt. Rev. Nirmol Vincent Gomes, Bishop of Krishnagar.

The school is legally registered under the Societies Registration Act XXVII 1961 (Registration No. S/17302). It reserves the right to run according to its norms to provide the highest standard of education.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

The vision is to foster holistic excellence by equipping students intellectually, emotionally, morally, and spiritually to face life’s challenges. It aims to shape them into competent professionals, enlightened leaders, responsible homemakers, and globally aware citizens.

Our Mission

The mission is to nurture a love for learning and leadership through quality education in an inspiring environment. It aims to build resilient, disciplined, and self-reliant individuals rooted in strong values and faith, inspired by the lives of Jesus Christ and Mother Teresa.

Moral Education

Being a Christian Institution, the classes of Christian Doctrine (Catechism) are compulsory. All the students of Mother Teresa School attend classes of moral Science/Value Education, which deals with moral principles, values and rules of conduct. A student must strive to attend quality of mind and heart and integrate into his/her life virtues such as honesty, sincerity, piety, compassion, generosity, love for nature and self-reliance.

Morality & Discipline

Special attention is given to maintaining high standards of morality and discipline within the school. Guardians are encouraged to actively collaborate with the school authorities by ensuring regular attendance and punctuality of their children.
