School Establishment and Administration

Mother Teresa School, a Private English Medium, Co- Educational Institute is situated at Mahishakola, Nazirpur, in the district of Nadia in West Bengal, established on 21st April, 2018 is administered by the Roman Catholic Diocese of Krishnagar legally registered under the Societies registration Act XXVI1961, dated 11th February 1976 holding registration No. S/17302.

It reserves to itself the right to run the school according to its norms and close the school if it is prevented in any way from doing so. It is run and managed through the leadership of the Principal Under the guidance of Rt. Rev. Nirmol Vincent Gomes, Bishop of Krishnagar.
