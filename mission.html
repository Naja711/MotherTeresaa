Our Mission

Helping the students to love, learn and lead, promoting quality education, inspiring resilient children, creating inspirational environment and nurturing active faith.

Above all, to make them good human beings by forming in them habit of virtue, discipline and self-efficiency following the life example of Jesus Christ and enriched by the words and deeds of St. Mother Teresa.
