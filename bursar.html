Message from Bursar

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

As the Bursar of Mother Teresa School, I am honored to serve a community committed to excellence and values. Our motto, “Let Your Light Shine,” inspires every child to discover and share their unique gifts.

We believe that each student carries a spark of greatness within. Our role is to nurture that light with care, discipline, and encouragement. Education here goes beyond textbooks and classrooms; we aim to shape responsible, compassionate, and confident individuals.

Our school environment promotes curiosity, creativity, and integrity. We ensure that resources are managed wisely for the benefit of every learner. Transparency and accountability guide our administration, and we strive to provide quality facilities that support holistic development.

Teachers and staff work together as a dedicated family. We encourage students to dream big and act with purpose. Every achievement, big or small, is celebrated with pride. We also teach the importance of kindness and service to others.

Our mission is rooted in strong moral and spiritual values. We prepare students not just for exams, but for life. Parents are valued partners in this journey of growth. Together, we build a future filled with hope and possibility.

Let every child’s light shine brightly in all they do. May our school continue to be a place where light transforms lives.

With blessings and best wishes,

The Bursar
