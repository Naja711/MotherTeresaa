Principal's Message

A Journey of Transformation and Growth

Fr. Johny Varghese, MPH

Principal

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers,

It gives me immense joy to welcome you to Mother Teresa School, Nazirpur—a place where education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge, but a journey of transformation, character formation, and holistic growth.

Our vision is to widen the scope of holistic excellence, ensuring that every student is equipped intellectually, emotionally, morally, and spiritually to face the challenges of life with confidence and courage.

At the heart of our mission lies a commitment to help our students love, learn, and lead. We strive to provide a nurturing and inspiring environment that promotes quality education, builds resilience, and fosters an active and meaningful faith.

Our school motto, “Let Your Light Shine,” reflects our firm belief that every child is uniquely gifted. Each student carries within them a light of talent, goodness, and potential. It is our responsibility as educators to nurture this light—encouraging them to grow in confidence, integrity, and compassion, and to become responsible contributors to society.

“Do not wait for leaders; do it alone, person to person.”
— St. Mother Teresa

This reminds us that true education empowers individuals to take initiative, serve others, and bring about change in small yet meaningful ways.

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world.”
— Nelson Mandela

These words reaffirm our belief that education is the foundation upon which a just and progressive society is built.

At Mother Teresa School, Nazirpur, we are committed to shaping young minds with wisdom, strengthening hearts with values, and guiding lives with purpose. Together—with the support of our dedicated teachers and cooperative parents—we continue this noble mission of forming individuals who will shine brightly in the world with knowledge, love, and service.

With blessings and best wishes,

Fr. Johny Varghese, MPH

Principal

Mother Teresa School, Nazirpur
